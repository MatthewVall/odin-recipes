German Apple Pancake

Description

This pancake is a good meal for special occasions.

The ingredients you will need:

  • 4 large eggs
  • half a cup flour
  • a spoon of sugar
  • half a teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 pich of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoon of melted butter
  • half tablespoon ground nutmeg
  • quarter cup of butter
  • half cup of white sugar
  • half a spoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 tart of apple
  1. Making the dough
    1. Combine eggs, flour, 1 tablespoon sugar, baking powder and salt.
    2. Gradually add milk, stril constantly
    3. Add vanilla, melted butter, nutmeg
    4. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven
  3. Pre-bake
    1. Melt butter and brush it up on the side
    2. Combine sugar, cinnamon, and a teaspoon of nutmeg in a bowl
    3. Line the pan with apple slices, sprinkle the remaining sugar over the appples
    4. Place pan overr medium-heat until the mixture starts bubles, than getnly pour batter mixture over the apples
  4. Bake
    1. Bake it for 15 minutes
    2. Reduce heat to 190 degree and bake for 10 more minutes
  5. Serve it!
