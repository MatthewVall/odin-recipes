Skillet Meatballs

Description

An easy-to-use guide on how to make meatballs.

The ingredients you will need:

  • a pound of ground beef
  • a large egg, beaten
  • a tablespoon of milk
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • onion
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of granulated garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly found black pepper
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablesppon butter

Steps

  1. In a small bowl beat the egg, add the milk, stir in breadcrumbs, let it sit for 1 minute, add the gound beef.
  2. Dice onion
  3. Add onion, parmesan, garlic, salt, pepper to the bowl of ground bee, combine mixture gently. If it's thougth add 1 spoon of milk. Make meatballs.
  4. Heat oil and butter in a heavy skillet, place meatballs in a layer with no crowding, move them every 2-3 minutes, until all sides are brown.
  5. Serve it with pasta and sauce.
