Homemade Lasagna

Description

An easy to make lasagna for the whole family!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Meat
    • half pound ground pork
    • half pound lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
    • Two tablespoon of parsley
    • one clove of garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
    • Dried basil
    • Dried oregano
    • Salt
    • Black papper
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
    • Parmesan
    • Shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
    1. Cooke the meat utnill it's browned and crumbly
    2. Add the onion, cook it till until it's translucent
    3. Stir in the canned tomato, half of parsley, garlic, 1,5 teaspoon of salt, organo and sugar
  2. Boil the noodles.
  3. Make the cheese layer.
    • Mix the cheeses, eggs, the remaining parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna
    • Start with the sauce, end with the mozzarella
  5. Bake the lasagna.
    1. Cover with foil
    2. Bake it in preheated oven for half an hour
    3. Remove foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
